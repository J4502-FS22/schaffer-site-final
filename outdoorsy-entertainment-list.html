7 Pieces of Entertainment to Get Into That Outdoorsy Mood

What is that free, loose feeling that comes with being in nature that we simply canâ€™t describe with words alone? Check out these seven pieces of entertainment, from books to comics, to get into the outdoorsy spirit and embrace that childlike feeling from playing outside.

Long Lost

Album by Lord Huron

Listen to Long Lost on Spotify

For their fourth studio album, indie-folk-rock band Lord Huron creates an eerie, nostalgic sound that is perfect for a chilly, cloudy hike in the woods. Just remember that if you do choose to listen to music while enjoying the outdoors, wear headphones! Do not create sound pollution that can disturb wildlife by playing music from a loud speaker.

Dear Wormwood

Album by The Oh Hellos

Listen to Dear Wormwood on Spotify

This perkier listening option will get you in the mood to grab a friend by the hand, run to the nearest Irish pub, and dance to the sound of live fiddles. Like Lord Huron, The Oh Hellos fall into the indie-folk-rock genre, but they invoke a completely different feeling with their energetic instruments and natural-sounding backup vocals.

Over the Garden Wall

Television series by Cartoon Network

Watch Over the Garden Wall on HBO Max

Despite this blog being about motivating you to go outside, you might be tempted not to leave your couch once you start watching "Over the Garden Wall." This surprisingly beautiful and emotional short series from Cartoon Network follows two brothers as they travel through the fantastical land of the Unknown. Bonus: For my "Lord of the Rings" fans out there, the main character is voiced by Elijah Wood. Sold.

Hilda

Television series by Netflix

Watch Hilda on Netflix

Netflix's "Hilda" is another cute, animated series whose main character journeys through magical lands and deals with fantastical creatures. Channel your inner adventurer like Hilda and explore the wonder of your own backyard.

Song of the Sea

Film by Tomm Moore

Watch Song of the Sea on Amazon Prime

Scuba diving, anyone? "Song of the Sea" makes you wish you could breathe underwater, talk to animals, and, most of all, live in Ireland. Follow siblings Ben and Saoirse as they try to save a world of Celtic faeries against a surprisingly relatable antagonist.

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

Film by Ben Stiller

Watch The Secret Life of Walter Mitty on HBO Max

Ah, Walter Mitty: every photographer's favorite movie. Few films will inspire you to seize the day and see the world quite like this one. Embrace your inner daydreamer and climb mountains with Ben Stiller in this quirky pick.

The Boneless Mercies

Book by April Genevieve Tucholke

Get The Boneless Mercies on Amazon

In this retelling of Beowulf, a gang of fierce, female warriors try to escape the "death trade" and find their own epic destinies. This book is the perfect read for sitting under a tree, chilling in a meadow, or laying in bed by the window light of the moon.
